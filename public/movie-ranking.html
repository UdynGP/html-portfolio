The Best Movies According to Udayan

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

The best sci-fi movie I have watched till date. An absolute masterpiece from Christopher Nolan.

Kung Fu Panda

One of the best inspirational movies according to me. It highlights the importance of belief in your abilities under the most adverse circumstances.

Kimi No Na Wa

A beautiful display of time travel. It shows Love as an emotion which can really cross three dimensions!
